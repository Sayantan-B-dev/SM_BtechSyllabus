Mean Deviation About Mean

Problem:
Find the Mean Deviation about Mean for the following grouped data.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
No. of Absentees | Frequency (fi) | Midpoint (xi) | xi × fi | |xi - x̄| × fi
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
0 - 5            |       6        |     2.5       |   15    | |2.5 - 12.67| × 6  = 61.02
5 - 10           |      14        |     7.5       |  105    | |7.5 - 12.67| × 14 = 72.38
10 - 15          |      20        |    12.5       |  250    | |12.5 - 12.67| × 20 = 3.40
15 - 20          |      12        |    17.5       |  210    | |17.5 - 12.67| × 12 = 57.96
20 - 25          |       8        |    22.5       |  180    | |22.5 - 12.67| × 8  = 78.64
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Calculate the Mean

Formula:
Mean (x̄) = Σ(xi × fi) / Σfi

Substitute the values:

Mean = (15 + 105 + 250 + 210 + 180) / (6 + 14 + 20 + 12 + 8)

Mean = 760 / 60

Mean = 12.67

------------------------------------------------------------

Step 2: Calculate Mean Deviation about Mean

Formula:
MD(mean) = Σ[fi × |xi - x̄|] / Σfi

Substitute the values:

MD(mean) =
(61.02 + 72.38 + 3.40 + 57.96 + 78.64) /
(6 + 14 + 20 + 12 + 8)

MD(mean) = 273.40 / 60

MD(mean) = 4.56 (approximately)

------------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer

Mean = 12.67

Mean Deviation about Mean = 4.56
