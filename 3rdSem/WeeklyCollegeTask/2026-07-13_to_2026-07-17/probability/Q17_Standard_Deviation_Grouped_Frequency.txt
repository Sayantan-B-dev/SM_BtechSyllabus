Q17. Identify the Standard Deviation of the following distribution.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Turnover (Rs.)      No. of Firms (f)
-------------------------------------------------------------
50 - 100                  5
100 - 150                 8
150 - 200                 9
200 - 250                12
250 - 300                18
300 - 350                23
350 - 400                17
-------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Find the class marks (midpoints)

-------------------------------------------------------------
Class Interval      f      Midpoint (x)      fx
-------------------------------------------------------------
50 - 100            5          75            375
100 - 150           8         125           1000
150 - 200           9         175           1575
200 - 250          12         225           2700
250 - 300          18         275           4950
300 - 350          23         325           7475
350 - 400          17         375           6375
-------------------------------------------------------------

Σf  = 92

Σfx = 24450

-------------------------------------------------------------

Step 2: Calculate the Mean

Mean (x̄) = Σfx / Σf

          = 24450 / 92

          = 265.76

-------------------------------------------------------------

Step 3: Calculate (x − x̄)² and f(x − x̄)²

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
x      f      x − x̄      (x − x̄)²          f(x − x̄)²
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
75     5      -190.76      36390.97          181954.84
125    8      -140.76      19813.88          158511.06
175    9       -90.76       8237.79           74140.08
225   12       -40.76       1661.71           19940.52
275   18         9.24         85.62            1541.13
325   23        59.24       3509.54           80719.46
375   17       109.24      11933.45          202868.64
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Σf(x − x̄)² = 719675.73

-------------------------------------------------------------

Step 4: Variance

σ² = Σf(x − x̄)² / Σf

   = 719675.73 / 92

   = 7822.25

-------------------------------------------------------------

Step 5: Standard Deviation

σ = √7822.25

σ = 88.44

-------------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer

Mean = 265.76

Variance = 7822.25

Standard Deviation = 88.44
