Q15. Calculate the Standard Deviation from the following table.

---------------------------------------------------------
X        10    20    30    40    50    60
f        19    25    32    35    14     7
---------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Find Σf

Σf = 19 + 25 + 32 + 35 + 14 + 7
    = 132

---------------------------------------------------------

Step 2: Calculate fx

---------------------------------------------------------
X      f      fx
---------------------------------------------------------
10     19     190
20     25     500
30     32     960
40     35    1400
50     14     700
60      7     420
---------------------------------------------------------

Σfx = 190 + 500 + 960 + 1400 + 700 + 420
     = 4170

---------------------------------------------------------

Step 3: Calculate Mean

Mean (x̄) = Σfx / Σf

         = 4170 / 132

         = 31.59

---------------------------------------------------------

Step 4: Calculate (X − x̄)² and f(X − x̄)²

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
X     f     X − x̄      (X − x̄)²      f(X − x̄)²
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10    19    -21.59        466.13          8856.47
20    25    -11.59        134.33          3358.25
30    32     -1.59          2.53            80.96
40    35      8.41         70.73          2475.55
50    14     18.41        338.93          4745.02
60     7     28.41        807.13          5649.91
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Σf(X − x̄)² = 25166.16

---------------------------------------------------------

Step 5: Variance

Variance (σ²)

= Σf(X − x̄)² / Σf

= 25166.16 / 132

= 190.65

---------------------------------------------------------

Step 6: Standard Deviation

σ = √190.65

σ = 13.81

---------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer

Mean = 31.59

Variance = 190.65

Standard Deviation = 13.81
